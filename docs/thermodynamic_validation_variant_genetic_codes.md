# Thermodynamic Validation of the Preservation Constraint Equation in Variant Genetic Codes

## An Independent Epistemological Assessment

### 1. Introduction

This document records the user-provided research narrative on the Preservation Constraint Equation (PCE) as applied to genetic-code degeneracy. The central claim is that the standard genetic code sits at a special zero-residual point under a coupled relationship between:

- degeneracy variance ($\sigma^2$), and
- effective wobble efficiency ($\tau$).

The report emphasizes an epistemological requirement: $\tau$ must be derived independently from thermodynamic and structural biology evidence rather than back-solved from the PCE.

### 2. Independent Biophysical Basis for Wobble Efficiency ($\tau$)

The argument assembles three lines of evidence:

1. **RNA thermodynamics** (especially terminal G·U and I·U wobble behavior),
2. **Ribosomal decoding-center stabilization** (A1492/A1493 induced-fit and A-minor contacts),
3. **tRNA wobble-base modifications** (position-34 chemistry and decoding expansion).

The synthesis proposes a near-unity but sub-maximal effective global wobble efficiency and uses:

\[
\tau \approx 0.985
\]

as an independently grounded operating constant for the standard nuclear code.

### 3. Standard Code Exactness (Sense-Codon Regime)

Using the canonical 61-sense-codon mapping to 20 amino acids:

- 1-fold: 2 amino acids
- 2-fold: 9 amino acids
- 3-fold: 1 amino acid
- 4-fold: 5 amino acids
- 6-fold: 3 amino acids

The report states a variance:

\[
\sigma^2 = 2.4475
\]

Substitution of $(\sigma^2,\tau)=(2.4475,0.985)$ into the PCE is claimed to produce a functionally zero residual.

### 4. Stop-Codon Symmetry-Breaking Control

When stop codons are included as a pseudo-amino-acid class, the report states that the PCE requires an unphysical:

\[
\tau > 1
\]

to restore zero residual. This is interpreted as a mathematical control demonstrating that PCE equilibrium applies to the **sense-codon subspace**, while stop codons act as biologically meaningful symmetry breakers.

### 5. Falsifiability via Variant Genetic Codes

The report proposes and applies a falsifiability criterion:

- Variant translation tables should break zero residual.
- Residual magnitude should track structural deviation from the universal code.

Case studies include:

- Vertebrate mitochondrial code (Table 2),

- Invertebrate mitochondrial code (Table 5),

### 6. Reported Pattern Across Evolutionary Contexts

The submitted analysis claims:

- **Standard universal code**: exact/near-exact zero residual,
- **Nuclear variants**: small but non-zero residual “topological scars,”
- **Mitochondrial minimized codes**: large non-zero residuals,
- **64-sense context-dependent systems**: require unphysical parameters under strict PCE assumptions.

### 7. Epistemological Position

The core position in the manuscript is that independent thermodynamic anchoring of $\tau$, followed by out-of-sample testing on naturally occurring variant code tables, elevates the framework from curve-fitting concern toward a falsifiable structural hypothesis.
